SEBI Registration Documentation Service

Introduction

SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) mandates that individuals and entities providing investment advisory or research services must obtain proper registration. This ensures transparency, investor protection, and regulatory compliance.

Who Needs This Service?

  • Research Analysts (RA): Professionals providing research reports, stock recommendations, or market analysis.
  • Registered Investment Advisors (RIA): Individuals or firms offering personalized investment advice for a fee.
  • Traders & Investment Professionals looking to legally offer advisory services.

Our service simplifies the SEBI registration process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience.

Required Documents

To ensure a smooth SEBI registration process, the following documents are required:

Category Documents
Personal & Business Details PAN Card (Personal & Business), Aadhaar Card, Passport-size Photograph, Address Proof
Qualification Certificates (for RIA) Postgraduate Degree in Finance, Business, Economics, or related field, NISM Investment Adviser Level 1 & Level 2 Certification / Research Analyst 15 Certification
Financial & Net Worth Statements 3 years Income Tax Returns (ITR), Bank Statements (last 6 months), Net Worth Certificate from a CA, CIBIL Report
Compliance Reports Business Plan & Advisory Model, Internal Risk & Compliance Policy
Other Regulatory Requirements SEBI Undertaking & Declarations, Legal Agreements (if applicable)
