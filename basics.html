Introduction to Stock Market Basics

The stock market is a dynamic financial ecosystem where individuals and institutions buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies. Whether you're a beginner looking to understand how markets work or an investor aiming to refine your strategies, this course is designed to equip you with the fundamental knowledge needed to navigate the stock market confidently.

In this course, you will learn:

  • The structure and functioning of stock exchanges.
  • How to select the right stocks based on research and analysis.
  • The importance of risk management and portfolio diversification.

By the end of this course, you'll have a strong foundation in stock market principles, enabling you to make informed investment decisions.
